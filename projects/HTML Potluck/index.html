Friday Night Movie Showcase

Haikyu!! The Dumpster Battle

"Haikyu!!: The Dumpster Battle" is a movie that continues the story of the popular volleyball anime series, focusing on the intense match between Karasuno High School and Nekoma High School, often referred to as "The Dumpster Battle" due to the teams' nickname. The film centers on the long-awaited face-off between the two schools, which have a history of friendly rivalry, and the stakes are high as both teams are determined to win.

Street Fighter 2 Animated Movie

"Street Fighter II: The Animated Movie" is a 1994 animated film adaptation of the iconic video game. It centers on the conflict between the world's best street fighters and a sinister organization, Shadoloo, led by the ruthless M. Bison. The film follows Ryu and Ken as they are drawn into Bison's plot, while Chun-Li and Guile work to stop him.

Spy X Family CODE: White

"Spy x Family Code: White" is a Japanese animated movie that's a spin-off of the anime series, but not a direct continuation of the story. It's a humorous and heartwarming adventure focusing on the Forger family – Loid, Yor, and Anya – while incorporating elements of spy and assassin missions. The movie follows the Forger family as they navigate a family vacation that leads to unexpected world-saving situations.
